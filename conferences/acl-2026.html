About ACL

ACL is the premier international conference in computational linguistics and natural language processing, bringing together researchers from academia and industry worldwide.

Research Areas

  • • Machine translation and multilingual NLP
  • • Language models and generation
  • • Information extraction and question answering
  • • Dialogue systems and conversational AI
  • • Computational linguistics and linguistic theory
  • • Ethics and bias in NLP
